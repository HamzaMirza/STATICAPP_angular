Systems and Options

The highest performing diagnostic systems specifically designed for electrical device analysis
of failed digital and mixed signal semiconductor devices.

DI Lab
System™

View DataSheet

Comprehensive Product Engineering and Failure Analysis Lab system for complete device analysis and physical defect isolation.

Configuration includes the complete suite of Teseda analysis tools.

System Configuration:

  • Diagnostic Test Hardware

    Purpose-built for silicon debug, scan-based diagnostics and Failure Analysis. 512 pins – 100MHz – 64MB per-pin pattern depth with flexible per-pin timing.

  • Teseda WorkBench™ Software

    Test control for the Teseda Diagnostic Test Hardware to perform scan-based testing, capture test results in pass/fail, failure capture and full capture modes of operation and display the test results graphically.

  • Parametric Measurement Unit

    Provides the engineer with a comprehensive suite of DC measurement capabilities.

  • Diagnostic Board

  • Defect Isolator™

    Maps scan test failures to the physical location on the die, reducing time to root-cause analysis, from weeks or months, to hours.

  • Broken Chain Analyzer™

    Automatically pinpoints defective flip-flop locations in broken scan chains.
